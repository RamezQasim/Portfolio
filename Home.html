Design and Analysis of a DC Nano Grid System (09/2019 - 12/2019)

Using MATLAB and Simulink, a DC Nano-Grid System fed by PV Array of cells was designed and simulated, PID controllers were designed to maintain the desired behaviour of the system. Additionally, MPPT algorithm was applied to increase the efficiency of the system.
This project was part of the Power Electronics course. Link to project

 ThermoBlast (2014 - 2015)

A wearable device that gives pulses of cooling or heating to individuals rather than cooling or heating the space (rooms/buildings). This project has won the second place in Microsoft Imagine Cup competition in Jordan, and the semi-grand award in an international competition in Indonesia. 

From conceiving the idea, to building the team and working on the prototype, Thermoblast had been a very enriching experience. The promotional video was an amateur attempt at highlighting the concept, there was also an amateur attempt at writing a business plan.
